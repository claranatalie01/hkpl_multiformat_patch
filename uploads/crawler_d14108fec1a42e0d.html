Improvement Works at Tsz Wan Shan Public Library [Expected Works Period: 22 May to late September 2026]


11.05.2026

Improvement works for smart book return will be carried out at Tsz Wan Shan Public Library from 22 May 2026 (Friday) and last for approximately four months.  The library will operate as usual during the works period.  Every effort will be made to minimise the noises or other nuisances generated by the works. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

For enquiries or assistance, please contact the library staff (Telephone: 2927 6050).
