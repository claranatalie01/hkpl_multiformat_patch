Hong Kong Public Libraries - Important Notices

About this Site

This website is produced and maintained by the Hong Kong Public Libraries (HKPL), Leisure and Cultural Services Department, the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China.

The site is best viewed with desktop version Microsoft Edge, Firefox, Chrome or Safari.

Web Accessibility Conformance Statement

This website adopts web accessibility design and conforms to the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 Level AA standard. Individual less commonly used webpages with archive materials may not incorporate all the accessibility features. You are welcome to contact us by email (libcu@lcsd.gov.hk ) if you wish to acquire the information in a more accessible format.
