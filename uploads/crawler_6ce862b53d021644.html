Borrowing Popular Good Reads@SUEP, SUEP “Hong Kong Renowned Writers” e-Book, SUEP e-Book and Jin Yong Martial Arts Novels Audio Collection with e-Readers:

You may use different types of e-readers to borrow Popular Good Reads@SUEP, SUEP “Hong Kong Renowned Writers” e-Book, SUEP e-Book and Jin Yong Martial Arts Novels Audio Collection.  The provision of the services is subject to the agreement between SUEP and individual e-readers.

  • “SUEP e-Book – Nowbook Digital Library” mobile app supports open source e-readers, including all “BOOX” e-reader models.


Borrowing Popular Good Reads@SUEP, SUEP “Hong Kong Renowned Writers” e-Book, SUEP e-Book and Jin Yong Martial Arts Novels Audio Collection with “BOOX” e-Readers:
  • Web Version (access via the “Nowbook Digital Library” e-Book platform)
  1. Enter the URL “https://www.hkpl.gov.hk/suep” in “NeoBrowser".

  1. Click “Continue” at the bottom of the e-Book description page.

  1. Read the Disclaimer and click “Accept”.

  1. Enter your library card or HKID card number and password and click “Login”.

  1. After logging into SUEP e-Book, click the three vertical dots at the upper right corner for more options and click “Add to Home screen”.

  1. A shortcut to the “Nowbook Digital Library” e-Book platform will be added to the home screen.  You can now log into the web version of SUEP e-Book via the “Apps” section easily.

 

  • Mobile App (access via the “SUEP e-Book – Nowbook Digital Library” mobile app)
  1. Download the “SUEP e-Book – Nowbook Digital Library” mobile app in your “BOOX” e-reader at Google Play Store.

  1. If your e-reader does not have Google Play Store, you can search for the APK file of “SUEP e-Book – Nowbook Digital Library” in a web browser for downloading and installation.

 

  1. Launch the “SUEP e-Book – Nowbook Digital Library” mobile app and click the user profile icon at the upper right corner to log in.  After reading the Disclaimer and clicking “Accept”, enter your library card or HKID card number and password and click “Login”.

 

  • Borrowing SUEP e-Book, SUEP “Hong Kong Renowned Writers” e-Book and Jin Yong Martial Arts Novels Audio Collection with “BOOX” e-Readers
  1. Select the e-book you are interested in on the homepage.  Click “Borrow” or “Instant Online Reading” on the introduction page of the e-book.

  1. If you want to borrow the e-book, click “Borrow" and “Confirm” in the pop-up window.  You can start reading or listening now.
