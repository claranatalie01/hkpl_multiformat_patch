Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 4 [Service Suspension Date: 02.07.2026 - 08.07.2026]


03.06.2026

 

Mobile Library 4 will suspend service from 2 July 2026 to 8 July 2026 for routine preventive maintenance.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause. 

The service points to be affected are as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Yat Tung Estate 06.07.2026 (Monday) & 08.07.2026 (Wednesday)
Discovery Bay 02.07.2026 (Thursday) & 07.07.2026 (Tuesday)
Pui O 03.07.2026 (Friday)
Shui Hau 04.07.2026 (Saturday) a.m. 
Tong Fuk 04.07.2026 (Saturday) p.m.

During the above period, readers can use various electronic library services through the Library website (http://www.hkpl.gov.hk).  Loan items can also be renewed by telephone on 2698 0002/ 2827 2833 or Library website.
