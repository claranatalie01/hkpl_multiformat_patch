Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 10 [Service Suspension Date: 14.07.2026 - 17.07.2026]


14.07.2026

Services provided by Mobile Library 10 would be suspended from 14 July 2026 (Tusday) to 17 July 2026 (Friday) due to urgent maintenance.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.  The service points to be affected are as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Gold Coast Piazza, Castle Peak Bay, Tuen Mun 14 July 2026 (Tusday) a.m.
Adjacent to Block 1, Lung Mun Oasis, Tuen Mun 14 July 2026 (Tusday) p.m. and 17 July 2026 (Friday)
Adjacent to Yuk Kwai House, Kwai Chung Estate, Kwai Chung 15 July 2026 (Wednesday)
Adjacent to Yuet Tai House, Tin Yuet Estate, Tin Shui Wai 16 July 2026 (Thursday)

During that period, the library materials borrowed from above service stops may be returned on next servicing day.  Loan items can also be renewed by telephone on 2698 0002 / 2827 2833 or online at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please contact our mobile library staff on 2450 1857.
