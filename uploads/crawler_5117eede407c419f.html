New service arrangements for Mobile Library 6


22.01.2026

 

The mobile library service point at Shau Kei Wan Market will cease service from 29 January 2026 (Thu) onwards. Readers may visit Yiu Tung Public Library, Mobile Library 9 service point at Oi Tung Estate or other public libraries in Eastern District to use library services.  Please refer to the following link for the location of public libraries in the Eastern District:

https://www.hkpl.gov.hk/en/locations/eastern/libraries.html

In addition, the service periods of mobile library service points at Heng Fa Chuen in Chai Wan and Tai Hong Street in Lei King Wan will be extended starting from 2 March 2026 (Mon).  Details are as follows:

Service Points Additional Service Periods
Adjacent to Block 6, Shing Man Lane, Heng Fa Chuen, Chai Wan Mondays
10:00 am – 1:00 pm
2:00 pm – 6:00 pm
(except public holidays)
Adjacent to Yat Sing Mansion, Tai Hong Street, Lei King Wan Wednesdays
10:00 am – 1:00 pm
2:00 pm – 6:00 pm
(except public holidays)

For details of Mobile Library 6 services, please call 2505 4690 or visit the website of the Hong Kong Public Libraries: www.hkpl.gov.hk.
