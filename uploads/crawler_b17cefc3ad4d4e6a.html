Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 1 [Service Suspension Date: 2.7.2026]


02.07.2026

Due to maintenance works at the parking space at Ko Chi House, Ko Yee Estate, the Mobile Library 1 will suspend its service on 2 July 2026 (Thursday) .We are sorry for the inconvenience caused. The service point to be affected are as follow:

Service Points

Suspension Date

Adjacent to Ko Chi House, Ko Yee Estate, Yau Tong

2 July 2026 (Thursday)

During the service suspension, members of the public are welcome to borrow and return library materials at other public libraries. They may also renew library materials by telephone at 2698 0002 or 2827 2833, or via the Internet at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk. For enquiries, please call 2414 3157.
