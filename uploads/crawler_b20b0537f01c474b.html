To maintain the quality of the collections of the Hong Kong Public Libraries (HKPL) and to ensure that the collections do not contain objectionable contents (such as those which exaggerate violence, pornography and vulgarism), violate the laws of Hong Kong or endanger national security, the HKPL has set up multiple channels for members of the public to express their views on library collections via online form, by email or in paper form.

You may submit the required information via the online form below or email the required information to bookreport@lcsd.gov.hk.

Paper forms are also available at any branch of the HKPL or downloadable here.

Please note the following:

  • The reported information is intended for handling library materials of the HKPL only;
  • The reported information is not intended for handling general enquiries, complaints or suggestions;
  • No replies will be given to you;
  • No follow-up action could be taken if you do not provide the information as required in the form.
  • The HKPL will not collect your personal data through online or paper forms. The data collected will be dealt with strictly in accordance with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance and will not be disclosed to any third parties.
