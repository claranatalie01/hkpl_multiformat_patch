Temporary suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 6 [Service Suspension Date: 01.06.2026-06.06.2026]


24.04.2026

Due to routine preventive maintenance of the mobile library van, Mobile Library 6 will suspend services on 01 June 2026 (Mon) to 06 June 2026(Sat). We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

The mobile library service points affected are as follows:

Mobile Stops Suspension Date
Adjacent to Block 6, Shing Man Lane, Heng Fa Chuen, Chai Wan 01 June (Mon)
Adjacent to Lei Tim House, Ap Lei Chau Estate 02 June (Tue)
Adjacent to Yat Sing Mansion, Tai Hong Street, Lei King Wan 03 June (Wed)
Adjacent to First Aid Station, Shek O Beach 04 June (Thu)
Adjacent to Tung Ping House, Lei Tung Estate, Ap Lei Chau 05 June (Fri)
Adjacent to Choi Lam House, Tsui Lam Estate, Tseung Kwan O 06 June (Sat)

Readers are welcome to use other public libraries during the service suspension. They may also renew library materials by telephone at 2698 0002 or 2827 2833, or via the Internet at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please call 2505 4690.
