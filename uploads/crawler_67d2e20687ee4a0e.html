Improvement Works at Butterfly Estate Public Library[Expected Works Period: 20 April to Mid-August 2026]


13.04.2026

To tie in with the implementation of the Smart Library System, improvement works for Smart Book Return will be carried out at Butterfly Estate Public Library from 20 April 2026 and approximately last for four months. During the works period, part of the Adult Library will be temporarily closed, while the other library facilities and services will be available as usual.

Every effort will be made to minimise noises or other nuisances arising from the works.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause. For enquiries or assistance, please contact our staff (Tel.: 2468 0518).
