Reroofing of waterproof layer at Ma On Shan Public Library (Important Notice) [Expected Works Period: 15.12.2025-15.06.2026]


01.12.2025

Reroofing of waterproof layer will be carried out at Ma On Shan Library starting from 15 December 2025. The works are expected to last for around six months. Noise and odour will likely occur during the works period, the contractor will try to control noise, odour and other nuisances arising from the works to within established standards and guidelines.  You may consider to use other study rooms or public libraries if required. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
