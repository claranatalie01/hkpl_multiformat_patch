What is e-Resources Full-text Search?

e-Resources Full-text Search is a one-stop search system for easy discovery of hundred thousands of full-text e-Books, audiobooks, journal articles, video clips and music soundtracks etc. from various Internet access e-Books and e-Databases provided by the Hong Kong Public Libraries.

What are the features?

  • Easy-to-use basic search
  • Search multiple e-Databases simultaneously
  • Display integrated results set
  • Automatic de-duplication of searching results
  • Full-text filter
  • Enter to e-Databases without re-logging in via the system
