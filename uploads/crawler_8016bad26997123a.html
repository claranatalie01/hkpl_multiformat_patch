Temporary Arrangement of Mobile Library Services at Tai Wo Hau Estate – Mobile Library 12


26.05.2026

 

Due to the external wall repair work near the parking area of Tai Wo Hau Estate, the mobile library service at Tai Wo Hau Estate will be suspended on 28 May 2026 and the service stop will be temporary relocated to Kwai Luen Estate (adjacent to Luen Yan House, Kwai Luen Estate, Kwai Chung) from 2:30pm to 6:00pm.

Readers are welcome to use the mobile library services. For enquiries, please contact our mobile library staff on 2479 1055.
