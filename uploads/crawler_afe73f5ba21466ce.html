My Library
The Official Mobile App

The official mobile application (“My Library”) of Hong Kong Public Libraries brings public library services to users in the mobile world at any time and any place. A number of popular functions are available on the mobile app which includes:

  • Access to and Management of multiple library accounts
  • Viewing of transaction history
  • Searching, reserving and renewing of library materials
  • Locating nearest libraries using GPS*, as well as address & contact information of all libraries
  • Personalized alert services, including pick-up of reserved items and due dates of borrowed books
  • Paying overdue fines and reservation fees by mobile payment services*
  • Saving and retrieving library materials in My Lists

For details, please use the ‘Help’ function from the menu of ‘My Library’.

* Not applicable to the special version downloaded in Huawei AppGallery
