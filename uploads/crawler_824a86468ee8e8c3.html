Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 10 [Service Suspension Date: 14.07.2026]

Services provided by Mobile Library 10 would be suspended on the morning of 14 July 2026 (Tusday) due to urgent maintenance.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.  The service points to be affected are as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Gold Coast Piazza,
Castle Peak Bay, Tuen Mun		 14 July 2026 (Tusday) a.m.
Adjacent to Block 1,
