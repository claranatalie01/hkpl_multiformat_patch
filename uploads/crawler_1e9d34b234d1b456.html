Partial Service Suspension of Self-service Library Station - Island East Sports Centre Sitting-out Area [Service Suspension Date: 25.12.2025 - 27.12.2025, 1.1.2026]


19.12.2025

Owing to maintenance work of the library system, this library station will suspend service during the following time:

  • From 00:30 am on 25 December, 2025 (Thursday) to 07:00 am on 27 December, 2025 (Saturday)
  • From 00:30 am to 11:59 pm on 01 January, 2025 (Thursday)

During the service suspension, readers can still return library materials at the Self-service Library Station, and loan records will be updated within two working days. We apologise for any inconvenience caused.
