Library Catalogue

The Library Catalogue enables you to search the entire Chinese and English collections of the Hong Kong Public Libraries. In addition to a wide range of search options, such as author, title, subject etc.,

you have the choice at the start to narrow your search to books only, to audio-visual materials only or to some other categories or materials.

From any Self-service Kiosk you will know instantly whether the item you need is available on the shelf or on loan, or being reserved, and if it is on loan, you will also know the date the item is due for return.

In case you wish to know library related activities that are being held or forthcoming; or wish to check your own borrowing record, like how many books you have borrowed, which item is overdue etc., simply follow the menu options and you will find them all. You may also perform renewal and reservation at any Self-service Kiosk.
