Improvement Works at Students’ Study Room of Lockhart Road Public Library [Expected Improvement works period: 07.2026 - 08.2026]


13.07.2026

Additional power sockets will be installed in the Students’ Study Room of the Lockhart Road Public Library starting from 20 July 2026. During the works period, part of the Students’ Study Room will be temporarily closed, while other library facilities and services will be available as usual. Every effort will be made to minimise noise or other nuisances arising from the works. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

For enquiries or assistance, please contact our staff at 2879 5560.
