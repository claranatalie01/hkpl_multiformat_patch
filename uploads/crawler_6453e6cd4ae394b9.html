Inspection of ceiling at Sham Shui Po Public Library [Expected Works Period: 8 to 11 June 2026]

Inspection of ceiling is carried out at Sham Shui Po Public Library starting from 8 June 2026 (Monday) for approximately four days. During the works period, part of the Children's Library and Multimedia Library are temporarily closed, while the other library facilities and services are available as usual.

Every effort is made to minimise noises or other nuisances arising from the works.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause. For enquiries or assistance, please contact our staff at 2234 5122.
