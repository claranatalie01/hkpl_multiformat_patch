Temporary suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 6 [Service Suspension Date: 11.07.2026]


11.07.2026

Due to urgent operational arrangement of the Mobile Library van, Mobile Library 6 will suspend services on  11 July 2026 (Saturday). We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

The mobile library service points affected are as follows:

Mobile Stops Suspension Date
Adjacent to Choi Lam House, Tsui Lam Estate, Tseung Kwan O, Sai Kung 11 Jul (Sat)

Readers are welcome to use other public libraries during the service suspension. They may also renew library materials by telephone at 2698 0002 or 2827 2833, or via the Internet at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please call 2505 4690.
