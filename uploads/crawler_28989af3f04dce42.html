Meet-the-Authors 2026: Blissful Den

Description Happiness can be sharing a feast with family, savoring the wonderful flavours of the world;
Happiness can be playing with children, reliving the fond moments of childhood;
Happiness can be offering treats to our feline friends, feeling satisfied of being a dutiful cataholic;
Happiness can be reading your favourite book, immersing yourself in the ocean of words.

What does happiness mean to you? In this year’s talk series, writers will visit the “Blissful Den” to share their insights on writing and reading. Let’s wander through the sea of books with them and find joys hidden between the pages.
Date 2026/4/18 (Saturday) to 2026/12/19 (Saturday)

1/Meet-the-Authors 2026: Blissful Den - What is Happiness?

Date & Time: 30 August 2026 (Sunday)
2:30 p.m. - 4:00 p.m.
Venue: Ping Shan Tin Shui Wai Public Library (Extension Activities Room)
Description: What is happiness? Can it be pursued? How can it be attained? In an era of constant overstimulation, how can we regain inner peace and achieve true happiness? The speaker will share stories of happiness based on psychological theories and his personal experience.

Date & Time: 18 April 2026 (Saturday)
2:30 p.m. - 4:00 p.m.

Description: Dr. Tim Leung, a senior STEAM education expert, will start with an optical experiment to debunk common scientific myths.
