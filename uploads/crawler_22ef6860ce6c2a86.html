New service arrangements for Mobile Library 5 – New service point at Fu Tip Estate, Tai Po [starting from 20.4.2026 onward]


24.03.2026

 

Starting from 20 April 2026 (Monday) onwards, a new service point for Mobile Library 5 will be set up at the area adjacent to Hei Tip House, Fu Tip Estate in Tai Po. The opening hours of the new service point will be from 2:45pm to 6pm every alternate Monday, except public holidays.

The opening hours of the existing service point adjacent to Heng Tsui House, Fu Heng Estate in Tai Po will be adjusted on the same day. The new opening hours will be from 2.30pm to 6pm every alternate Friday, except public holidays. For enquiries about Mobile Library 5 services, please call 2696 5842.
