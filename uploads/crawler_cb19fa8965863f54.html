* PDF e-Form

Under the Electronic Transactions Ordinance, patrons may make electronic submission to the Government. Apart from downloading the conventional forms [PDF] and submitting them in paper form, you may choose to download the PDF e-Form which facilitates you to fill-in and submit the forms and documentary proof via email to hkplcru@lcsd.gov.hk.

For using the PDF e-Form submission, you need to have:

1. Computer Software: Adobe Acrobat Reader DC / 2017 or other software that can fill in and save PDF e-Forms.
2. e-Certificate: For signing of e-Form electronically. For details of e-Cert, please visit the related web page of the Hong Kong Post at http://www.hongkongpost.gov.hk
3. Documentary Proof Scanned PDF files for the following documents (Maximum size up to 10MB).

  • Proof of applicant’s identity
  • Guarantor’s Hong Kong Identity Card (if applicable)
  • Proof of residential address (if applicable)
