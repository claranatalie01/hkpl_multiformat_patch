Welcome to “Modern Living” website!

The website provides the general public the library resources about the “Modern Living” collection set up at the Tsuen Wan Public Library. It serves to promote public’s awareness and further to ignite their interest to pursue trends of interior decoration of house, smart home and ways to green living.
