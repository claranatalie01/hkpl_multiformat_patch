Reading Made Easy!

The Hong Kong Public Libraries has a rich collection of local publications covering a wide range of categories, including award winning books, children and young adult collections, popular novels, food and travel guides, etc. We have gradually added book covers and summary to the local publications in the Library Catalogue, so as to provide more detailed bibliographic information for you to borrow and reserve your favourite reading materials and enjoy the fun of reading.
