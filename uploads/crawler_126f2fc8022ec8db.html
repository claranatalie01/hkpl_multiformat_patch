Discontinuation of the “INFOBANK” Online Database Service

As the vendor ceases its business operations in Hong Kong, the “INFOBANK” online database service provided by Hong Kong Public Libraries has been discontinued from 1 June 2026. Readers may access other e-databases for Chinese business-related information via Hong Kong Public Libraries webpage.

Launch of "OverDrive Chinese eBooks (Simplified Chinese)" services

The Hong Kong Public Libraries has launched the OverDrive Chinese eBooks (Simplified Chinese) service since 30 December 2025. This e-books collection offers a selection of simplified Chinese e-books, covering popular fictions from Chinese Mainland as well as good reads for children and youths. Apart from online reading with library accounts on personal computers or mobile devices anytime and anywhere, readers may also download e-books for offline reading via the Libby mobile app. For details of the OverDrive Chinese e-Books (Simplified Chinese) service, please visit the e-book webpage.

 (30.12.2025)

 

Launch of "Popular Good Reads@SUEP" services

The Hong Kong Public Libraries launches the Popular Good Reads@SUEP service on 28 February 2025. A collection of selected popular e-books in traditional Chinese from the "Nowbook Digital Library" e-book platform, covering such topics as literature and fiction, household knowledge, spiritual support, humanities and social science, business and economics, as well as medicines and healthcare. For details of this e-book service, please visit the e-book webpage.

(08.03.2025)

 

Launch of "OverDrive Chinese eBooks (Traditional Chinese)" services

The Hong Kong Public Libraries has launched the OverDrive Chinese eBooks (Traditional Chinese) service since 20 December 2024. This e-books collection offers a variety of Chinese e-books, covering such topics as literature and fiction, spiritual support, household knowledge, technology and medicines, business and economics, social sciences, as well as history and biographies. Good reads for children and youths are also included. Apart from online reading with library accounts on personal computers or mobile devices anytime and anywhere, readers may also download e-books for offline reading via the Libby mobile app. For details of the OverDrive Chinese e-Books (Traditional Chinese) service, please visit the e-book webpage.
