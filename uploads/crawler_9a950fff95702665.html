Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 2 [Service Suspension Date: 24-26.06.2026]


26.06.2026

Due to urgent maintenance of the mobile library van, the library service of Mobile Library 2 will be suspended on the following date.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

The service points to be affected are as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Maple Street Playground, Maple Street, Sham Shui Po 24.06.2026 (Wednesday)
Adjacent to Po Ning House(West), Po Lam Estate, Tseung Kwan O 25.06.2026 (Thursday)
Adjacent to Mei Tao House, Mei Lam Estate, Sha Tin 26.06.2026 (Friday)
 
During the affected period, readers may borrow and return library materials at other Hong Kong Public Libraries.  Readers may also renew their library materials by telephone renewal at 2698 0002 or 2827 2833, or by Internet renewal (www.hkpl.gov.hk). 
 
For enquiries, please contact us at 2605 1071.
