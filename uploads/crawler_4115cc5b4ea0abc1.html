Welcome to ‘Sports and Fitness’ Information !

The 'Sports and Fitness’ Information is the web-based extension of the ‘Sports and Fitness’ Collection of Sha Tin Public Library. The ‘Sports and Fitness’ Collection which served as the focal point in the Hong Kong Public Libraries (HKPL) helps readers explore HKPL's resources and WWW information on sports and fitness.
