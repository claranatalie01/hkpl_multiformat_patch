Library Barrier-free Facilities

To cater to the needs of all individuals in using library facilities and services, as well as participating in various library activities, the Hong Kong Public Libraries provide the following accessible facilities:

 (1) Automatic doors (2) Accessible lifts (3) Braille and tactile floor plans
(4) Accessible toilets (5) Tactile guide paths (6) Staircase with braille and tactile information on handrail Accessible toilets
(7) Lower service counters (8) Floor standing type self-checkout machines (9) Desktop type self-service kiosks
(10) Barrier-free computer facilities (11) Induction loop systems (12) Audio and visual signals of the emergency alarm systems
