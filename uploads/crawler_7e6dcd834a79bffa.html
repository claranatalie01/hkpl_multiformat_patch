The real-time loan record update function at Sham Shui Po Public Library will be temporarily suspended from 5:00 p.m. on 26 April to 12:00 noon on 27 April 2026


26.04.2026

Owing to the inspection for the building’s  electrical Installations, the real-time loan record update function at Sham Shui Po Public Library will be temporarily suspended from 5:00 p.m. on 26 April to 12:00 noon on 27 April 2026. Readers may still return library materials to the book return facilities at G/F and 1/F and the loan record will be updated by 27.4.2026.
