Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 2 [Service Suspension Date: 13.10.2023 PM until further notice]


13.10.2023

Due to renovation works in Sui Wo Court, Mobile Library 2 will suspend service from 13.10.2023 PM until further notice.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

The service point to be affected is as follow:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Wing Mau House, Sui Wo Court, Sha Tin 13.10.2023 (Friday) PM until further notice

Readers are welcome to use other public libraries during the aforesaid service suspension period.  They may also renew library materials by phone at 2698 0002 or 2827 2833, or via the Internet at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.  For enquiries, please call 2605 1071.
