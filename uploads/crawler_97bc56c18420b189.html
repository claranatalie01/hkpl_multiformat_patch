Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 10 [Service Suspension Date: 24.06.2026]


03.06.2026

The library services provided by Mobile Library 10 will be suspended in the morning on 24 June 2026 (Wednesday) due to temporary electricity suspension at Wang Ngai House, Cheung Wang Estate. The library services will be resumed at 2:00 p.m. on the same date. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

The service point to be affected is as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Wang Ngai House, Cheung Wang Estate, Tsing Yi

10:00 a.m. to 01:00 p.m. on 24 June 2026
(Wednesday)
(Library services will resume at 2:00 p.m. on the same day.)

During that period, the library materials borrowed from the above service stop may be returned on next servicing day. Loan items can also be renewed by telephone on 2698 0002 / 2827 2833 or online at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please contact our mobile library staff on 2450 1857.
