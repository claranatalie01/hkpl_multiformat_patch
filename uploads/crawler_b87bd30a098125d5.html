Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 11 [Service Suspension Date: 15.07.2026-21.07.2026]


02.06.2026

 Mobile Library 11 will suspend services from 15 to 21 July 2026 for routine maintenance.  We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
 The service points to be affected are as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date
Adjacent to Kwong Yuen Community Hall, Kwong Yuen Estate, Sha Tin 15.07.2026 (Wednesday)
Adjacent to Ching Wai House, Cheung Ching Estate, Tsing Yi 16.07.2026 (Thursday)
Loading Bay adjacent to Tower 1, Easeful Court, Tsing Yi 17.07.2026 (Friday)
Adjacent to the carpark entrance of the Ancillary Facilities Block, Tin Wah Estate, Tin Shui Wai 18.07.2026 (Saturday)
Chung Ling Sheh Carpark, Cho Yiu Chuen, Kwai Chung 20.07.2026 (Monday)
Adjacent to Block 4, Po Tin Estate, Tuen Mun 21.07.2026 (Tuesday)

During that period, the library materials borrowed from above service stops may be returned on next servicing day. Loan items can also be renewed by telephone on 2698 0002 / 2827 2833 or online at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please contact our mobile library staff on 2479 1055.
