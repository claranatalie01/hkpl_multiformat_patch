Temporary Suspension of Mobile Library Services – Mobile Library 12 [Service Suspension Date: 16.07.2026]


16.07.2026

Due to urgent maintenance of the mobile library van, Mobile Library 12 will suspend services on 16 July 2026. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

The service point to be affected is as follows:

Service Point Suspension Date

Adjacent to Luen Yan House, Kwai Luen Estate, Kwai Chung

16.07.2026 (Thursday)

During the above period, readers who need to borrow or return library materials may visit other public libraries.  Loan items can also be renewed by telephone on 2698 0002 / 2827 2833 or online at http://www.hkpl.gov.hk.

For enquiries, please contact our mobile library staff on 2479 1055.
